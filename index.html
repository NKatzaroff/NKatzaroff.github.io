What I'm Building

Health technology built from personal experience — not a trend.

💧

Sponge Hydration

Co-Founder & CEO · Founded June 2024

Visit Sponge →

Sponge is a smart hydration system — a compact magnetic device that clips onto any water bottle and automatically records water intake, paired with an app that tracks your history, builds streaks, and locks your favorite apps (Instagram, TikTok) until you hit your daily goal. No manual logging. No willpower required.

We built it because both founders watched family members affected by dementia and cognitive decline — and discovered how much proper hydration matters for long-term brain health. First batch shipped April 2026. Available on iOS, Android, and at spongehydration.com.
